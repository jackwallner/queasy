FAQ

Do I need an Apple Watch? It's best with one, but no: hold your iPhone against your inner wrist and Queasy plays the same pattern with the phone's haptics.

Is this a medical device? No. Queasy is a drug-free comfort aid inspired by P6 acupressure wristbands. If nausea is severe or persistent, see a doctor.

Where does my data live? On your devices. No account, no ads, no tracking. Privacy policy.
