Queasy Privacy Policy

Effective July 1, 2026

Queasy is built to keep your data on your devices. This policy explains what little we handle and why.

What Queasy stores

Your symptom check-ins, relief sessions, and settings are stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch. They are never uploaded to our servers. We do not operate servers that receive your health information, and Queasy does not read or write Apple Health data.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to validate purchases and understand aggregate subscription performance. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and purchase receipts, not your name, email, or symptom data. See the RevenueCat privacy policy.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, no sign-in
  • No ads and no ad tracking
  • No sale or sharing of personal data
  • No reading of Apple Health data

Deleting your data

Delete the app and its local data is gone. Purchase records remain with Apple per their policies.

Medical disclaimer

Queasy is a drug-free comfort aid, not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or cure any condition. If nausea is severe or persistent, consult a doctor. If you are pregnant, check with your OB or midwife.
